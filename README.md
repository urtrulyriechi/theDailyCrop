# The DailyCrop.

**DailyCrop** is a seasonal subscription service delivering fresh, locally sourced fruits and vegetables straight to your home.  
Designed as a student project, it blends clean UI design with sustainable living values.

---

## ✨ Features
- 🥕 **Fresh & Local**: Produce straight from nearby farms  
- 🌍 **Sustainably Sourced**: Good for you, good for the planet  
- 💚 **100% Satisfaction**: Happiness in every box  
- 🚚 **Free Delivery**: Fast and secure  

---

## 📦 Our Boxes
- **Solo Harvest Box (Small)** → Perfect for 1–2 people  
- **Family Fresh Box (Medium)** → Balanced mix for households  
- **Community Feast Box (Large)** → Generous box for sharing  

---

## 🛒 How It Works
1. Pick your box size  
2. Customize your harvest (fruits, veggies, or mix)  
3. Choose one‑time, weekly, or monthly subscription  
4. Enjoy doorstep delivery
